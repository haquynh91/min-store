The Magformers Landmark Set is a colorful magnetic construction set with 174 pieces for building eight 3-D structures of famous landmarks, some as tall as two feet. The pieces come in a variety of different shapes, and each one can attach together on any side using built-in magnets. The instruction booklet shows how to build the Leaning Tower of Pisa, the Space Needle, the Eiffel Tower, the Empire State Building, the Sydney Opera House, the Great Pyramid, the Golden Gate Bridge, and Tower Bridge. You probably won't have enough pieces to build all eight structures at once, so you'll most likely have to build one at a time. Of course, kids can also use the pieces to build whatever they want.
